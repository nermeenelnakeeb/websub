Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Renting a bike is easy! You can book online through our website or mobile app, or visit one of our rental stations. You'll need a valid ID and payment method. After selecting your bike and rental duration, you'll receive a confirmation with pickup instructions.

Our rental stations are typically open from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. Some locations may have extended hours during peak seasons. Bike returns can be made 24/7 at any of our automated stations.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal for online transactions. At our physical locations, we also accept cash payments.

Late returns will incur additional charges. For hourly rentals, you'll be charged for an extra hour if you're up to 30 minutes late, and a full day rate if more than 30 minutes late. For daily rentals, each additional hour is charged at 20% of the daily rate.

Yes! We offer special rates for weekly and monthly rentals. Weekly rentals save you 15% compared to daily rates, and monthly rentals save 30%. Contact our customer service for custom long-term rental arrangements.
